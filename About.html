About Me

I'm a self-motivated, lifelong student with a passion for computer science. Currently based out of Cincinnati Ohio and am currently enrolled in the software development program at Cincinnati State, I often use programming in my personal hobbies and most of them involve some form of creation. I'm part artist and part logical thinker. I'm eagerly looking for either a Co-Op role, contract, or something more permanent.

How Am I Different?

Determined. Optimistic. Energetic. All words that I would describe myself with. We all have our fair shares of short comings and I'm no different. But what makes me different? I'm constantly and forever on a mission to improve myself and to grow. I'm always learning something and always trying my hardest. While some people may slow down on learning about the subject they're researching, I just don't. I can't. I always find myself with the need to know more.

My Interests

I enjoy making pixel art, writing code, and taking short walks on the beach. Making games has become a big passion of mine because of how it blends all the hobbies I've ever had into one project. I occasionally participate in game jam events and have built up my skills from the limitations they use. I often build things with arduino as well! I have been mostly a guitar player my whole life but I can play a wide array of stringed instruments. At one point I was even enrolled in a dual major for conventional and classical guitar performance but eventually backed out in order to pursue greater things. Some of my favorites on the list of projects I'm currently undertaking are building an arduino based puzzle box with both electronic and traditional puzzle elements, game development projects and the art behind them, a smart mirror, and some API bots to manage my crypto trading for me (check back and eventually you may see some tutorials here!).
